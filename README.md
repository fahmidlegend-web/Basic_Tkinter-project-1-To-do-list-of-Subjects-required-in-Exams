# Basic_Tkinter-project-1-To-do-list-of-Subjects-required-in-Exams
Its a very basic ,beginner app made in Tkinter. This app consists of several features such as adding,deleting,updating,searching tasks. You can also view your tasks by show buttton.
This app can help you update your tasks, you can also mark it as done or not .You can view your tasks and add parameters related to your task(by add key) to load extra information . You can also put data related to your parameters(be it subject_code , percentage obtained in last exams or even topics of your subject anything you wish ) .
